App Features

Interactive Music Sheets

Follow along with visual music sheets that highlight notes as you play, making learning intuitive and engaging.

Progressive Difficulty

Start with beginner levels and advance through intermediate to expert pieces at your own pace.

Audio Playback

Listen to high-quality recordings of each piece to perfect your technique and timing.

Learn Fundamentals

Master the basics of Ney playing with detailed tutorials on posture, breathing, and embouchure.

Offline Access

Download music sheets and audio files to practice anywhere, even without an internet connection.

Traditional Wisdom

Learn from master Neyzen Cemil Baştürk's expertise, preserving centuries of musical tradition.

About NeyzenCe

"Bişnev in ney çün şikâyet mî küned, Ez cüdâyîhâ hikâyet mî küned."

- Mevlâna Celâleddin-i Rûmi -

Listen to how the ney complains, telling tales of separation.

Dear Ney enthusiasts, we have prepared the NeyzenCe app hoping to provide you with relative ease in the challenging journey of learning the Ney.

It should not be forgotten that the Ney, in its most beautiful and correct form, should be learned through apprenticeship with a master teacher. Our aim is only to support those who cannot find this opportunity for various reasons.

May your breath say "Hû". With our respect, greetings, and love.
