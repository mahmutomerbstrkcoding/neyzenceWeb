Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 5, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to NeyzenCe. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use certain features, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Profile information
  • Authentication information from third-party providers (Google, Apple)

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you use the App:

  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Learning progress and completed lessons
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App performance and crash reports

2.3 Analytics Information

We use analytics services (including Mixpanel) to understand app usage and improve our services. This may include:

  • Session duration and frequency
  • Feature usage statistics
  • User engagement metrics
  • Language preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important notifications about the App
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party services that help us operate the App (analytics, authentication, cloud storage)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with several third-party services:

  • Supabase: For backend services and user authentication
  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Sign-In: For authentication (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Mixpanel: For analytics (subject to Mixpanel's Privacy Policy)

Please review the privacy policies of these services as they may collect and use information independently.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request account deletion at any time.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our App may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance functionality and collect usage information. You can control cookie settings through your device preferences.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Privacy & Data Protection FAQ

What personal data does NeyzenCe collect?

We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality: email for account creation, progress data for learning tracking, and usage analytics to improve the app experience. We do not access your microphone, camera, or personal files without explicit permission.

How is my learning progress data stored?

Your progress data is securely stored using Supabase with end-to-end encryption. This includes completed lessons, achievement scores, and personalized settings. Data is backed up regularly and accessible only to you.

Can I download my data from NeyzenCe?

Yes! Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to data portability. Contact us at info@neyzence.com to request a complete export of your account data in a standard format.

How long do you keep my personal information?

We keep your data only as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Learning content and music remain available as they are educational resources.

Do you share data with music industry partners?

No, we do not share personal user data with any music industry partners or third parties for marketing purposes. Your learning journey and musical preferences remain private and are used only to enhance your NeyzenCe experience.

How do you protect children's privacy?

NeyzenCe follows strict COPPA guidelines for users under 13. We require parental consent and collect minimal data for children. Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's data at any time.
